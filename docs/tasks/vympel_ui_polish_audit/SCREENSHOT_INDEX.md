## Runtime measurements referenced by the specification

- Catalog inactive search relative to `.catalog-toolbar-shell`: 19/38 at 1440 and 1920; 30/38 at 768, 900, 1000, 1024, 1100, and 1280.
- Catalog active search at 1024: top 30 px, left/right 42 px, width 818 px.
- Product active thumbnail: approximately 0.8 px border plus 2 px active shadow in computed style.
- Description links: 130 px top margin at 1440 and 390.
- Related → Contact: 120 px at 1440; 64 px at 390.
- Cart heading top gap: 72 px at 1440; 56 px at 390.
- InfoPage section padding-bottom: 170 px at 390/320; with Footer margin the effective content gap is 234 px.
- Sort options: 16 px font, 22 px line-height, 56 px mobile row height.
- Mobile PIERRE RICAUD pill at 320: `justify-content: normal`, `text-align: start`, `white-space: nowrap`, clipped/truncated text.
